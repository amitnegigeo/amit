About Me

Hello! I'm Negi Amit Singh, a dedicated professional with a strong foundation in Remote Sensing and GIS Applications, currently pursuing an M.Tech in Disaster Mitigation and Management at IIT Roorkee.

My expertise lies in operating GIS software, managing geodatabases, and generating spatial databases of natural and manmade resources. I have practical experience in cadastral mapping, georeferencing, digitization, and spatial analysis of land parcels.

I am passionate about leveraging geospatial data and advanced techniques like machine learning for real-world applications, including flood detection, LULC classification, and hydrodynamic modeling for disaster risk assessment. My goal is to contribute to data-driven decision-making and strategic planning in environmental and disaster management contexts.
